ABOUT LAZY BONES

Meet the team behind your favorite meal planning tool

Our Story

Lazy Bones Meal Prep was born from a simple idea: nutrition shouldn't be complicated. Our founder, a fitness enthusiast who hated counting calories, wanted to create a tool that made healthy eating accessible to everyone, especially those who consider themselves "too lazy" to meal prep.

Since our launch in 2020, we've helped thousands of people transform their eating habits without the hassle of complex nutrition plans. Our philosophy is simple - good nutrition should be easy, convenient, and enjoyable for everyone.

Today, Lazy Bones continues to grow with a community of users who share our passion for simplified nutrition and balanced living.

OUR MISSION

The values that drive everything we do

Simplicity

We believe nutrition should be simple. Our tools strip away complexity and deliver exactly what you need without the fluff.

Balance

Good nutrition isn't about perfection—it's about balance. We help you find sustainable eating patterns that work with your lifestyle.

Accessibility

Healthy eating should be for everyone. We create tools that are easy to use regardless of your nutrition knowledge or cooking skills.
